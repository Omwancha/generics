ABOUT

The Generics is a musical band which came to existence in the early 90's, when some friends decided to take their friendship to the next level......they always thought that friendships are to be kept for the good and the bad that we may face while trying to be loyal to our buddies....

Music isn't just a combination of words for us...it's all about the good moments that we all always together trying to figure out wat to do only to find yourself speechless since you had nothing to say...having someone who can always be there matters..

Our music mostly tries to tell people of their daily struggles and trying to see to good in people in though it might be hard to do so....all "ships" die but friendships always wins.....
